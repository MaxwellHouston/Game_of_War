How to Play

  1. Play starts by spliting a deck of cards in two, and you and your opponent each get half.
  2. Place one card off the top of your deck on the table and the opponent does this as well.
  3. In case of a tie, each player puts down two addition cards face down, and a third one face up.
  4. Repeat step 3 if necessary.
  5. Whoever has the highest card wins the hand.
  6. The winner takes all cards on the table and puts them at the bottom of their deck.
  7. Once one player is completly out of cards, the game is over.
