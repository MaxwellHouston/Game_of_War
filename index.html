How to Play

  1. You play by spliting a deck of cards in two, and you and your opponent each get half.
    For now it's just a computer
  2. Draw one card off the top of your deck and the opponent does as well.
  3. Whoever has the highest card wins the hand.
  4. The winner takes both cards and puts them at the bottom of their deck.
  5. Once one player is completly out of cards, the game is over.
